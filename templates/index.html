Why This Matters

Cystitis and bladder cancer often present with similar symptoms such as hematuria, making early differentiation challenging. Misdiagnosis can delay critical treatment.

This AI-driven system assists clinicians by analyzing both medical images and laboratory parameters, improving diagnostic confidence and supporting clinical decision-making.
