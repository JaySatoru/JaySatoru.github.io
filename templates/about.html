About the Project

Deep Learning-Based Differentiation of Cystitis and Bladder Cancer

Project Overview

This project aims to differentiate Cystitis and Bladder Cancer using a multimodal deep learning approach. The system integrates medical image analysis with clinical laboratory parameters to assist in early and accurate diagnosis.

Medical Background

Cystitis

Cystitis is inflammation of the bladder, usually caused by infection. Symptoms may include pain, burning during urination, and blood in urine.

Bladder Cancer

Bladder cancer involves abnormal growth of cells in the bladder lining. Early symptoms may resemble cystitis, making differentiation challenging.

Methodology

The system uses EfficientNetB1 for image feature extraction and fully connected layers for clinical data processing. Both branches are combined to produce a final prediction.

Grad-CAM visualization is used to highlight image regions that influence classification decisions, making the model explainable.
